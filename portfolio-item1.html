Project Title - Travel Weather App

Project Date - May 2022

Project Owner - Christian Noa

About the Project

I love to travel and I also like to know exactly when to expect a change of weather at my destination. Although there's a variety of weather apps out there, I wanted to revolutionize my experience. I employed my proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Project Goals

Once at the airport, I came up with an idea to create a quick weather app that comprehensively showcases the temperature of my next destination, Miami. I aimed to test for:

The core functionality of the API.

Performance in case of expected and unexpected requests.

My favorite part of this project is the changing of the images when you enter a city in the search box which has simplified data reading.
